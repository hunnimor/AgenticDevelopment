## Skill: Architecture Decision

### Goal

Produce a clear technical decision with rationale, alternatives, and implementation impact.

### Steps

1. Read relevant sections of `PRD.md` and existing architecture docs.
2. Define the exact decision to make (one decision per run).
3. List 2-3 viable options.
4. Evaluate each option by:
   - Complexity
   - Scalability
   - Maintainability
   - Cost
   - Risk
5. Choose a recommendation and explain why.
6. Define migration/implementation plan and rollback strategy.
7. Share outputs with orchestrator and affected agents.
